Responsible Marketing

Marketing that’s truthful, transparent, and respectful.

Fedora Technologies follows responsible marketing practices inspired by leading industry principles, with a focus on honesty, human dignity, privacy, and clarity.

Principles

How we communicate

We aim to make marketing communication clear, substantiated, and respectful across all channels.

01

Truthful and substantiated

We avoid misleading claims and strive to support statements with evidence where applicable.

02

Transparent and clearly labeled

Commercial intent should be clear, especially for endorsements, testimonials, or native content.

03

Inclusive and respectful

We respect human dignity and avoid discriminatory messaging or imagery.

Data

Privacy and choice

We respect user choice and aim to minimize data collection. Where data is used, it should be used responsibly and securely, in line with applicable laws and best practices.

Purpose and clarity

If personal data is collected, we aim to explain why and how it will be used.

Security and privacy

We favor “privacy by design” and security-by-default approaches.

Opt-out and preferences

We respect requests to stop receiving marketing communications.

Read privacy overview

AI

Responsible use of automation and AI

When we use automation or AI in delivery or analysis, we aim for fairness, reliability, privacy, transparency, and accountability.

Fairness

We work to avoid bias and favor inclusive-by-design approaches.

Reliability

We validate outputs, test systems, and keep humans accountable for decisions.

Transparency

We aim to be clear about automated decision-making where it impacts users.

Sustainability

No greenwashing

Environmental claims should be specific, accurate, and evidence-based. We avoid vague or misleading sustainability statements.
